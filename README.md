## Command list

- ##### `djadvice 🐢`: Get Oogway's wisdom.
- ##### `djanime <anime> 😳`: Search for anime.
- ##### `djcat 🐱`: Random cute cat.
- ##### `djface 👩`: Random face.
- ##### `djhelp 🆘`: Show help.
- ##### `djmeme 🌈`: Random meme.
- ##### `djnamir 🐅`: Play "Wa namir \*amel".
- ##### `djping 🏓`: Show your ping.
- ##### `djspawn 🙋‍♂️`: Spawn your friends.
- ##### `djurban <word> 📚`: Search in urban dictionnary.
